# BETA TEST SCRIPT
**MimoNotes — Observing Customer Behavior**

**Date:** 2026-06-18
**Goal:** Watch real customers use the product. Don't help. Just observe.

---

## 1. TEST SETUP

### What You Need
- Screen recording software (OBS or built-in)
- Notebook for observations
- Timer (measure time-to-value)

### What the Customer Needs
- Laptop/phone with browser
- Their business documents (price list, catalog, FAQ)
- 30 minutes of uninterrupted time
- Their WhatsApp number

### Your Role
**You are an observer, NOT a helper.**

If they get stuck:
- Don't help immediately
- Write down where they got stuck
- Ask "What were you trying to do?" after they give up
- Only help after 2 minutes of struggle

---

## 2. TEST FLOW (30 Minutes)

### Phase 1: Signup (5 min)
| Step | Action | What to Watch |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Go to mimotes.ekohomelab.online | First impression, confusion? |
| 2 | Click "Daftar" | Do they find it? |
| 3 | Enter email + password | Any friction? |
| 4 | Dashboard loads | What's their first reaction? |

**Record:** Time to complete signup, errors, first words/reaction

### Phase 2: Onboarding Wizard (10 min)
| Step | Action | What to Watch |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Business name | Do they know what to put? |
| 2 | Business description | Do they write naturally or freeze? |
| 3 | WhatsApp number | Do they know their own number? |
| 4 | Upload document | Do they have a document ready? |
| 5 | Test chatbot | Do they ask a real question? |
| 6 | Publish widget | Do they understand embed code? |

**Record:** Time per step, hesitation points, questions asked

### Phase 3: First Conversation (5 min)
| Step | Action | What to Watch |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Open widget | Do they know how to embed? |
| 2 | Ask a question | Do they ask a real business question? |
| 3 | Get answer | Do they trust the answer? |
| 4 | Check sources | Do they look at where the answer came from? |

### Phase 4: Lead Capture (5 min)
| Step | Action | What to Watch |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Bot asks for contact | Do they provide real info? |
| 2 | Lead appears in dashboard | Do they find it? |
| 3 | WhatsApp button appears | Do they click it? |

### Phase 5: Wrap-up (5 min)
| Step | Action | What to Watch |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | "What did you think?" | First impression |
| 2 | "What was confusing?" | Pain points |
| 3 | "Would you use this?" | Validation |
| 4 | "How much would you pay?" | Pricing signal |
