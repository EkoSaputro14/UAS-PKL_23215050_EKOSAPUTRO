## 1. WHY THIS MATTERS

We built the product. Now we need to answer:
- Will anyone pay for this?
- How much will they pay?
- What problem does it actually solve for them?

**The biggest risk is not technical — it's building something nobody wants.**

## 3. RECRUITMENT STRATEGY

### Channel 1: Instagram DM (Primary)
```
Template:
"Halo [Nama], saya lihat bisnis [jenis]nya di Instagram. 
Kebetulan saya lagi riset tools chatbot AI yang bisa bantu 
jawab pertanyaan customer otomatis lewat WhatsApp. 

Boleh saya tanya beberapa hal soal customer service bisnisnya? 
Gratis, cuma 10 menit."
```

### Channel 2: WhatsApp Direct (Secondary)
- Find businesses from Google Maps
- Send WhatsApp message with the same template
- Include link to mimotes.ekohomelab.online

### Channel 3: Local Business Communities
- Facebook Groups: "Komunitas Bisnis [Kota]"
- WhatsApp Groups: Local UMKM groups
- LinkedIn: Indonesian SMB founders

### Channel 4: Referral
- Ask each tested customer to refer 1-2 others
- Offer 1 month free Pro for successful referral

## 4. SELECTION CRITERIA

### Must Have (ALL):
- [ ] Active business (not hobby/side project)
- [ ] Has WhatsApp Business or uses WhatsApp for customer service
- [ ] Gets at least 10 customer inquiries per week
- [ ] Owner is decision-maker (not employee)
- [ ] Willing to spend 30 minutes for onboarding + test

### Nice to Have:
- [ ] Already tried some form of automation (auto-reply, chatbot)
- [ ] Has existing documents (catalog, price list, FAQ)
- [ ] Active on social media (Instagram, Facebook)
- [ ] Located in Tier 2-3 cities (less competition, more receptive)

### Disqualifiers:
- [ ] Large enterprise (already has CS team)
- [ ] Government/NGO (different procurement process)
- [ ] No digital presence at all (too early)

## 8. RISKS & MITIGATIONS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Nobody responds to DMs | Medium | High | Use warm intros, local language, offer value first |
| Customers can't complete onboarding | Medium | High | Screen share support, simplify wizard |
| Product is too complex | Medium | High | Simplify, add video tutorials |
| Price is too high | Medium | Medium | Test lower prices, offer annual discount |
| Customers don't see value | Low | Very High | Focus on lead capture demo, show ROI |
