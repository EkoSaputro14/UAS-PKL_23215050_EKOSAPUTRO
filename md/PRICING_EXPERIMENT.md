# PRICING EXPERIMENT
**MimoNotes — Testing Willingness to Pay**

**Date:** 2026-06-18
**Goal:** Find the right price point for Indonesian SMBs

---

## 1. HYPOTHESIS

Indonesian SMBs will pay Rp 299K-799K/month for an AI chatbot that:
- Answers customer questions automatically
- Captures leads
- Works on WhatsApp
- Doesn't require technical skills

---

## 3. TESTING METHODOLOGY

### Method 1: Direct Ask (Interview)
```
"Mau bayar berapa?"
→ Follow up: "Kalau 299rb? 499rb? 799rb?"
→ Record highest price they say yes to
```

### Method 2: A/B Landing Page
| Group | Price Shown | Conversion |

### Method 3: Stripe Checkout Test
- Create 3 Stripe products (299K, 499K, 799K)
- Show price on dashboard
- Track who clicks "Upgrade"
- Track who completes payment

### Method 4: Annual Discount Test
| Monthly | Annual (per month) | Savings |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| 299K | 249K | 17% |
| 499K | 399K | 20% |
| 799K | 649K | 19% |

## 4. SUCCESS CRITERIA

| Metric | 299K | 499K | 799K |
|--------|------|------|------|
| Interest (interview) | > 60% | > 40% | > 20% |
| Click "Upgrade" | > 30% | > 20% | > 10% |
| Complete payment | > 20% | > 15% | > 5% |
| Annual preference | > 40% | > 40% | > 40% |

---

## 5. DECISION MATRIX

| Result | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 299K highest conversion | Launch at 299K, upsell later |
| 499K highest revenue | Launch at 499K, sweet spot |
| 799K most profitable | Launch at 799K, premium brand |
| All similar | Default to 499K (middle ground) |
| Nobody pays | Reconsider value proposition |

---

## 6. PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

### Anchoring
- Show 799K first, then 499K looks cheap
- "Save 20% with annual" makes monthly look expensive
- "Most popular" badge on 499K

### Objection Responses
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Terlalu mahal" | "Satu customer baru = 10x lipat biaya" |
| "Bisa gratisan" | "Gratis tidak ada support, tidak ada lead capture" |
| "Saya pakai WA sendiri" | "Jam 2 malam ada inquiry, siapa yang jawab?" |
| "Tidak paham teknologi" | "Saya bantu setup, tinggal pakai" |
