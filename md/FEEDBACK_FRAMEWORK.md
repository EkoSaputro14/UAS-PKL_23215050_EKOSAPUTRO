# FEEDBACK FRAMEWORK
**MimoNotes — Customer Interview Guide**

**Date:** 2026-06-18
**Goal:** Understand customer needs, objections, and willingness to pay

---

## 1. OPENING (2 min)
```
"Terima kasih sudah mau waktu untuk ini. Saya sedang riset 
tentang bagaimana UMKM Indonesia handle customer service. 
Saya mau dengar pengalaman Bapak/Ibu, bukan menjual produk. 
Jawaban jujur sangat membantu."
```

---

## 2. CORE QUESTIONS

### Problem Discovery
| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 1 | "Cara handle pertanyaan customer sekarang?" | Current workflow |
| 2 | "Berapa lama customer nunggu jawaban?" | Pain quantification |
| 3 | "Pernah ada customer kabur karena lama jawab?" | Revenue impact |
| 4 | "Berapa jam sehari habis balas chat?" | Time cost |
| 5 | "Pernah miss inquiry karena terlalu banyak?" | Missed opportunity |

### Solution Validation
| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 6 | "Kalau ada yang jawab otomatis 24/7, menarik?" | Interest signal |
| 7 | "Tapi jawabannya harus akurat?" | Quality concern |
| 8 | "Kalau langsung kirim ke WhatsApp?" | Handoff value |
| 9 | "Kalau bisa lihat siapa tanya dan apa yang dicari?" | Intelligence value |
| 10 | "Mau coba gratis 30 hari?" | Conversion signal |

### Pricing
| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 11 | "Mau bayar berapa?" | Open pricing |
| 12 | "Kalau 299rb sebulan, worth it?" | Price anchor low |
| 13 | "Kalau 499rb sebulan, masih oke?" | Price anchor mid |
| 14 | "Kalau 799rb sebulan, masih mau?" | Price anchor high |
| 15 | "Bayar tahunan lebih murah, suka mana?" | Annual vs monthly |

### Objection Handling
| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 16 | "Apa yang bikin ragu?" | Surface objections |
| 17 | "Sudah coba chatbot lain?" | Competition |
| 18 | "Kenapa tidak pakai lagi?" | Failure modes |
| 19 | "Apa yang bikin chatbot gagal?" | Quality requirements |
| 20 | "Apa yang bikin ini berhasil?" | Success criteria |

---

## 3. FOLLOW-UP BY OBJECTION

### "Sudah Pakai WhatsApp"
```
"Bagaimana handle inquiry malam hari? 
Atau saat sedang sibuk?"
```

### "Terlalu Mahal"
```
"Berapa rata-rata satu customer baru worth? 
Kalau tools ini dapat 1 customer baru per bulan, 
499rb masih mahal?"
```

### "Tidak Paham Teknologi"
```
"Kalau saya bantu setup, setelah itu tinggal pakai, 
apakah lebih mudah?"
```

### "Sudah Ada CS/Staff"
```
"Berapa gaji staff yang handle chat? 
Kurangi 1 orang shift malam, worth it?"
```

---

## 4. SCORING RUBRIC

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Problem Severity** | Tidak masalah | Agak ribet | Sangat memakan waktu |
| **Solution Interest** | Tidak tertarik | Mungkin dicoba | Sangat tertarik |
| **Willingness to Pay** | Tidak mau bayar | Mau kalau murah | Mau bayar sekarang |
| **Tech Readiness** | Tidak paham | Bisa pakai HP | Tech-savvy |

### Total Score:
- **16-20:** 🔥 Hot lead — close immediately
- **12-15:** ⭐ Warm lead — follow up with demo
- **8-11:** 🌡️ Lukewarm — nurture with content
- **4-7:** ❄️ Cold — not ready yet

---

## 6. INTERVIEW TIPS

### Do:
- ✅ Listen more than talk (80/20)
- ✅ Ask "why?" after every answer
- ✅ Note exact words they use
- ✅ Record interview (with permission)

### Don't:
- ❌ Sell during interview
- ❌ Defend product when criticized
- ❌ Promise features that don't exist
- ❌ Rush through questions
